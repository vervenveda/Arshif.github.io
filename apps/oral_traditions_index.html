Indigenous Oral Traditions Portal

A respectful learning hall for oral histories, storywork, songs, chants, genealogy, land memory, law, ecological knowledge, and living traditions from Indigenous communities around the world.

Protocol covenant: this portal teaches public context, source pathways, ethical research habits, and respectful comparison. It does not claim to own, reproduce, unlock, or authorize restricted sacred knowledge. When a tradition is living, local community guidance comes first.
