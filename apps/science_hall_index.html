History of Science Hall

A comprehensive academy portal for the long human story of discovery: sky, number, body, matter, life, Earth, machine, mind, society, data, medicine, climate, technology, and the ethics of knowledge.

This portal treats science as a global, revised, human practice. It includes laboratories and equations, but also fieldwork, craft, instruments, Indigenous and place-based knowledge, public health, translation, archives, visualization, institutions, and the social responsibilities of discovery.
