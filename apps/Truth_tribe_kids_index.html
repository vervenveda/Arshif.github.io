Choose a Path to Explore

Tap a button above to learn about a faith family.

Each card will tell you a little bit about what that group believes about God or the Divine, how they tell the story of creation, and how they try to live in a kind and loving way.

What You’ll See

  • Names they use for God or the Holy.
  • Their special books or stories.
  • A short peek at how they tell the creation story.

How to Use This

  • Read together as a family or class.
  • Talk about what feels similar or different to your own faith.
  • Remember: learning about others can help us be kinder neighbors.

Truth Tribe Reminder: No single card can explain everything about a faith. People inside each tradition may describe things in different ways.

Quiz & Reflection Hub

This sample quiz bank is gentle and big-picture, focused on respect and understanding. Use it as a starting point; feel free to adapt wording for your context.

Click “Start Quiz” to begin a short 5-question check-in.

    Reflection Prompts

    Copy/paste these into journals, slides, or exit tickets. Adjust language for your grade band.

    • What is one kind or beautiful idea you heard today from another faith’s story?
    • What is something your own family or tradition teaches that feels similar?
    • How can learning about other people’s beliefs help us be better neighbors or classmates?
    • If you could ask someone from one of these traditions a respectful question, what would you ask?
    • What is one way you can show kindness or care for creation this week?
