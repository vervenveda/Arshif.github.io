Course Posture Statement

We approach Scripture, history, and tradition with humility, honesty, and reverence. Each week we distinguish between (1) what the text says, (2) what external sources attest, and (3) what remains debated. Our aim is careful reading, fair evidence-handling, and faithful application— without forcing conclusions beyond the data.
