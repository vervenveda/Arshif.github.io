Course Posture Statement

We will: follow manuscript evidence, separate fact from inference, and treat all traditions respectfully.
This course examines how biblical texts were copied, translated, and canonized. When variants exist, we will: (1) identify the variant, (2) compare the evidence, (3) summarize what scholars can responsibly conclude, and (4) apply it to a faithful prayer life without claiming motives we cannot prove.
