Certification Path

ARSHIF Certificate Requirements

  • Complete all 48 learning modules.
  • Earn an average of 80% or higher on module quizzes.
  • Pass the midterm with 80% or higher.
  • Pass the final exam with 80% or higher.
  • Submit the capstone management plan.
  • Accept the ethics and professional-boundary pledge.

Mastery & Retake Policy

A score below 80% means “not yet.” Learners may review the relevant module, improve their artifact, retake the quiz or exam, and preserve the highest score. This encourages mastery instead of punishment.

Suggested pacing: 12-week intensive, 20-week semester, 40-week school-year, or open adult-learning path.
