    Observation Protocol

    1. Place: name, older names, Indigenous names when known.
    2. Route: how did you arrive, what path did you follow?
    3. Evidence: what did you see, hear, smell, touch, measure, or verify?
    4. Context: what history sits beneath the present scene?
    5. Ethics: whose privacy, sacredness, labor, or grief must be respected?
    6. Revision: replace assumptions with sourced context.
