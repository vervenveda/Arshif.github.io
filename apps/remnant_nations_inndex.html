Course Posture Statement:
We will not equate any modern nation-state with the Kingdom of God. In the New Covenant, God’s people are identified by faith, humility, repentance, and allegiance to Yahushua (Jesus).
We learn from Israel’s story as warning and witness, but we follow Messiah as our perfect example. Gentile inclusion is real, and Scripture strictly forbids pride or contempt: we reject boasting and honor God’s mercy toward all peoples.
